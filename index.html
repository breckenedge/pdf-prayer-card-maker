Prayer Card Tiler

Turn a 2-page prayer-card PDF (front image + back prayer) into a print-ready, n-up duplex sheet on US Letter — for printing your own prayer cards at home. Works with any 2-page card PDF.

Drop a 2-page PDF here
or click to choose a file

Printer margin: most consumer printers can't print within ~0.25" of the paper edge — set 0 only for a borderless printer. Gutter: blank space between adjacent cards for cutting room (set 0 to butt cards edge-to-edge).

Page 1 of your PDF is the card front, page 2 is the back. The back sheet is mirrored horizontally so cards register when you flip the printed sheet on its long edge (duplex, flip-on-long-edge).
